My Story

My journey began in retail at a photography studio at the mall. I worked there while I was both attending Community College of Philadelphia for Art & Design and raising my child as a young, single mom. This portion of my career taught me so much about the customer experience, working in fast-paced environments, and managing teams while simultaneously balancing other high-priority endeavors.

My customer service experience got my foot in the door at an awesome email marketing software company called AWeber as a support specialist. In this role, I quickly realized the customer demand for support designing and developing emails, one of the few areas where we did not offer support. I began to learn about email design and development, and eventually we began to operate an agency that we ran directly out of the customer support department. This not only created an additional revenue stream for our company, but more importantly, created better experiences for our customers.

Later I joined the design team at AWeber where I focused on in-house marketing, maintaining our customer-facing email template library, overseeing the email template agency in our customer support department, as well as working with various product development teams.

In 2018 I began picking up additional contract work with a Salesforce and Pardot implementation agency called Sercante. After a few months, I joined the team full-time to work on in-house marketing, client-facing projects, and building out our processes and team.
