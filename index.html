My Story

Like many people, my career journey has been untraditional.

It began in retail at a photography studio at the mall. I worked there while I was attending Community College of Philadelphia for Art & Design and raising my child as a young, single mom. This portion of my career has taught me so much about the customer experience, working in fast-paced environments, and managing teams while simultaneously balancing school work and raising a child.

My customer service experience got my foot in the door at an email marketing software company as a support specialist. In this role, I realized the customer demand for support designing and developing emails. I began to learn about email design and development, and eventually, we began to operate an agency that we ran directly out of the customer support department. Not only did this create an additional revenue stream for our company, but it created better experiences for our customers and opened up learning opportunities for other team members.

Eventually I joined the design team. I focused on in-house marketing, maintaining our customer-facing email template library, overseeing the email template agency in our customer support department, as well as working with various product development teams on email-related projects. In this role I began to branch out into other areas of design and development.

In 2018 I began working with a Salesforce and Pardot consulting agency where I worked on various types of client-facing projects, in-house marketing, and assisted in building processes and developing the team.
